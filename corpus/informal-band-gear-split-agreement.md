# Band gear split — Grey Creek Session

Me (Devon) and Priya and Marcus and JT are the four people in Grey Creek as of April 2026. We're putting this in writing now because we're buying the Tascam Model 24 together and we want to not have the same fight we had about the old van.

Total cost of the Model 24 + the two extra mics + cables: $2,186.44. We're splitting this four ways. Each person owes $546.61. Priya is fronting the whole thing on her card and everyone else will Venmo her their share by end of April.

Who owns it:
- The 4 of us, equal quarter shares.
- If somebody leaves the band, they get bought out at depreciated value. "Depreciated value" = whatever the band can reasonably sell it for on Reverb, minus 10%. We'll eyeball this. If we can't agree, we get 3 quotes and take the middle.
- Nobody can sell it without the other 3 saying yes.
- It lives at the practice space unless somebody needs it for a side gig. First come first served on side gigs. If two people want it the same night, flip a coin.
- If it gets stolen or wrecked while somebody was using it solo, that person is on the hook unless it was a freak thing (fire, act of god, etc). Insurance should cover most of it — Marcus is adding it to his renters policy, cost to be shared.

If the band breaks up (hope not): whoever wants it most can buy out the others at the depreciated value. If two people want it we flip a coin to see who gets first dibs.

This isn't a real contract but it's what we agreed to around JT's kitchen table on April 3rd. We all signed below. The signed physical copy lives in the band binder; a scan is in the shared Google Drive.
