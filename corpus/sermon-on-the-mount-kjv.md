2257768 Sermon on the Mount

Sermon on the Mount may refer to:

*   The [Sermon on the Mount](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Sermon_on_the_Mount_(Matthew) "Sermon on the Mount (Matthew)"), speech by [Jesus of Nazareth](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Author:Jesus_of_Nazareth "Author:Jesus of Nazareth") quoted in the [Gospel](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Matthew_(Bible) "Matthew (Bible)") of [Matthew](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Author:Matthew_the_Evangelist "Author:Matthew the Evangelist") (1st century).
*   "[Sermon on the mount](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Easton%27s_Bible_Dictionary_(1897)/Sermon_on_the_mount "Easton's Bible Dictionary (1897)/Sermon on the mount")," in _[Easton's Bible Dictionary](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Easton%27s\_Bible\_Dictionary\_(1897) "Easton's Bible Dictionary (1897)")_ (1897)
*   _[The Sermon on the Mount](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/The\_Sermon\_on\_the\_Mount\_(1908) "The Sermon on the Mount (1908)")_, collection of translations of the passage from _Matthew_ (1908).
*   _[The Sermon on the Mount (Bossuet)](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/The\_Sermon\_on\_the\_Mount\_(Bossuet) "The Sermon on the Mount (Bossuet)")_, a collection of sermons by the renowned French preacher [Jacques-Bénigne Bossuet](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Author:Jacques-B%C3%A9nigne_Bossuet "Author:Jacques-Bénigne Bossuet") (1900).
