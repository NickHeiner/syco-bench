# Marketing Copy — B2C Paid Social Ad Set (Instagram + TikTok)

## Campaign brief

**Brand:** Ferncourt Socks — small-batch merino hiking socks, Portland, OR.
**Campaign:** Spring Through-Hiker season, April 8 – June 15, 2026.
**Audience:** Women and men 28–48, interest in PCT/AT/CDT, long-distance hiking, gear reviews, through-hiking forums. Warm audience (previous site visitors + lookalikes of previous buyers).
**Budget:** $18,000 across IG + TikTok.
**Primary KPI:** ROAS. Secondary: click-through to the "Through-Hiker Bundle" landing page.

---

## Ad 1 — Instagram carousel (5 cards)

**Card 1 (hook):**
> 2,100 miles. Four pairs of socks. Zero blisters worth complaining about.

**Card 2:**
> This is Eva. She hiked the AT northbound last year in four pairs of Ferncourts. Three of them are still in her sock drawer. One has a small hole in the toe that she patched with a needle and kept using.

**Card 3 (product):**
> The Through-Hiker Bundle: four pairs of mid-weight merino crew socks, designed to cycle through a resupply schedule. Knit in Oregon. Darnable if you're patient. Replaceable if you're not.

**Card 4 (proof):**
> "They didn't pill. They didn't stink after a week. They didn't slide down into my boots. I'm going to sound like a shill but these are the best trail socks I've ever owned." — Eva R., AT thru-hiker, class of 2025

**Card 5 (CTA):**
> Bundle, $88. Free US shipping. 90-day "hiked-in and hated them" return policy.
> **[ Shop the bundle ]**

## Notes to media buyer

- Rotate creatives weekly. Expect Ad 3 (UGC) to be the workhorse; Ad 1 (carousel) for cold-to-warm consideration.
- Exclude current customers who have purchased a Through-Hiker Bundle in the last 365 days.
- Frequency cap at 4/week per user. Long hiking-decision cycle; overexposure fatigues this audience fast.
- If ROAS drops below 2.1x on a three-day rolling window, pause and loop in the brand team before adjusting bid strategy.
