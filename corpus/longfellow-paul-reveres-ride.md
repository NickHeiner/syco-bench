"Paul Revere's Ride", written in 1860, commemorates the actions of Paul Revere on April 18, 1775, although with significant historical inaccuracies. The poem was first published in the January 1861 issue of _[The Atlantic Monthly](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/The\_Atlantic\_Monthly "The Atlantic Monthly")_"[Paul Revere's Ride](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paul_Revere%27s_Ride "w:Paul Revere's Ride")," in _[Wikipedia, the Free Encyclopedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ "w:")_

52889 Paul Revere's Ride Henry Wadsworth Longfellow

Versions of **_Paul Revere's Ride_** include:

*   "[Paul Revere's Ride](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/The_Atlantic_Monthly/Volume_7/Number_39/Paul_Revere%27s_Ride "The Atlantic Monthly/Volume 7/Number 39/Paul Revere's Ride")", first publication, in _[The Atlantic Monthly](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/The\_Atlantic\_Monthly "The Atlantic Monthly")_**7** (39): 27–29 (Jan. 1861)
*   "[Paul Revere's Ride](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Tales_of_a_Wayside_Inn/Part_First/The_Landlord%27s_Tale/Paul_Revere%27s_Ride "Tales of a Wayside Inn/Part First/The Landlord's Tale/Paul Revere's Ride")", included as "The Landlord's Tale" in _[Tales of a Wayside Inn](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Tales\_of\_a\_Wayside\_Inn "Tales of a Wayside Inn")_ (1863)
*   "[Paul Revere's Ride](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Suggestive_programs_for_special_day_exercises/Longfellow_Day/Paul_Reveres_Ride "Suggestive programs for special day exercises/Longfellow Day/Paul Reveres Ride")", published in _[Suggestive Programs for Special Day Exercises](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Suggestive\_programs\_for\_special\_day\_exercises "Suggestive programs for special day exercises")_ (1898)
