### Song lyrics

Several sets of lyrics have been written to the tune known as "Auld Lang Syne":

*   "[Old Long Syne](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Old_Long_Syne "Old Long Syne")", the oldest known lyrics (anonymous)
*   "[Auld Lang Syne](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Auld_Lang_Syne_(Burns) "Auld Lang Syne (Burns)")", the most widely known lyrics, by [Robert Burns](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Author:Robert_Burns_(1759-1796) "Author:Robert Burns (1759-1796)")
*   "[Auld Lang Syne](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Auld_Lang_Syne_(Ramsay) "Auld Lang Syne (Ramsay)")", alternate lyrics by [Allan Ramsay](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Author:Allan_Ramsay "Author:Allan Ramsay")
