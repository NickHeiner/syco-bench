Original text from an illuminated manuscript by [William Blake](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Author:William_Blake "Author:William Blake"), Printed and published by Blake in [Songs of Experience](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Songs_of_Experience "Songs of Experience") (later in [Songs of Innocence and Experience](https://en.wikisource.org/wiki/Songs_of_Innocence_and_Experience "Songs of Innocence and Experience")), it is perhaps the most anthologised poem in the English language.
