## Research Interests

Cellular decision-making during regeneration. Specifically, how cells integrate positional, temporal, and mechanical cues to commit to differentiation trajectories in planarian and zebrafish models. Current projects investigate how noise in Wnt ligand gradients is filtered downstream, and whether this filtering is a general design principle or a system-specific feature.
