# TinyLlama: An Open-Source Small Language Model

**Authors:** Peiyuan Zhang, Guangtao Zeng, Tianduo Wang, Wei Lu

**arXiv:2401.02385** (cs.CL) — Submitted 4 Jan 2024

## Abstract

We present TinyLlama, a compact 1.1B language model pretrained on around 1 trillion tokens for approximately 3 epochs. Building on the architecture and tokenizer of Llama 2, TinyLlama leverages various advances contributed by the open-source community (e.g., FlashAttention and Lit-GPT), achieving better computational efficiency. Despite its relatively small size, TinyLlama demonstrates remarkable performance in a series of downstream tasks. It significantly outperforms existing open-source language models with comparable sizes. Our model checkpoints and code are publicly available on GitHub.

Subjects: Computation and Language (cs.CL).
